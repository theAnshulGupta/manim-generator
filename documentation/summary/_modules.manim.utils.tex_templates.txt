The `manim.utils.tex_templates` module provides a collection of LaTeX templates through two main classes: `TexTemplateLibrary` and `TexFontTemplates`. `TexTemplateLibrary` offers default and basic TeX templates with predefined preambles, while `TexFontTemplates` provides a wide range of font-specific templates that can be used when rendering TeX and MathTex objects, supporting various font styles, encoding, and math symbol representations.
