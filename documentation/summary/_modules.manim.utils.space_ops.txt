Here's a summary of the key functions in the `manim.utils.space_ops` module:

Vector Operations:
- `normalize(vect)`: Normalizes a vector to unit length
- `cross(v1, v2)`: Computes cross product of 3D vectors
- `angle_between_vectors(v1, v2)`: Calculates angle between two vectors
- `rotate_vector(vector, angle, axis)`: Rotates a vector around a specified axis

Coordinate Transformations:
- `cartesian_to_spherical(vec)`: Converts Cartesian coordinates to spherical
- `spherical_to_cartesian(spherical)`: Converts spherical coordinates to Cartesian
- `complex_to_R3(complex_num)`: Converts complex number to 3D vector
- `R3_to_complex(point)`: Converts 3D vector to complex number

Geometric Utilities:
- `midpoint(point1, point2)`: Calculates midpoint between two points
- `line_intersection(line1, line2)`: Finds intersection point of two lines
- `regular_vertices(n, radius)`: Generates regular polygon vertices
- `compass_directions(n)`: Generates evenly spaced directions

Rotation and Quaternion Functions:
- `quaternion_mult(...)`: Multiplies quaternions
- `quaternion_from_angle_axis(angle, axis)`: Creates quaternion from angle and axis
- `rotation_matrix(angle, axis)`: Generates rotation matrix

Polygon/Triangulation:
- `shoelace(x_y)`: Computes polygon area using shoelace formula
- `earclip_triangulation(verts, ring_ends)`: Triangulates polygons with potential holes
