The `manim.mobject.logo` module provides the `ManimBanner` class, which represents Manim's logo and banner. It allows creating and animating the Manim logo with customizable theme (dark or light), and offers methods like `create()` for generating the logo and `expand()` for transforming the logo into a full banner with the text "anim". The logo consists of geometric shapes (triangle, square, circle) and text letters, which can be animated with various effects like spiral creation and expansion.
