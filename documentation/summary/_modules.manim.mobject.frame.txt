The `manim.mobject.frame` module provides two rectangle classes for screen representation: `ScreenRectangle` and `FullScreenRectangle`. `ScreenRectangle` creates a rectangle with a configurable aspect ratio (default 16:9) and height, allowing dynamic width adjustment. `FullScreenRectangle` inherits from `ScreenRectangle` and automatically sets its height to the frame height specified in the Manim configuration.
