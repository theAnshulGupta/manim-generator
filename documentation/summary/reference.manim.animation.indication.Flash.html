Here's a concise summary of the Flash animation in Manim: Flash is an animation that creates lines radiating outward from a central point, simulating a flash effect. Key Parameters: - point: Center of the flash (can be a mobject or coordinates) - line_length: Length of flash lines (default 0.2) - num_lines: Number of radiating lines (default 12) - flash_radius: Distance from center where lines start (default 0.1) - line_stroke_width: Thickness of lines (default 3) - color: Color of lines (default yellow) - time_width: Duration of line appearance - run_time: Total animation duration Example Usage: ```python # Flash on a dot dot = Dot(color=YELLOW).shift(DOWN) self.play(Flash(dot)) # Flash on a circle with custom parameters circle = Circle(radius=2) self.play(Flash( circle, line_length=1, num_lines=30, color=RED, flash_radius=circle.radius+SMALL_BUFF )) ``` This animation creates a radial burst of lines from a specified point, useful for drawing attention or creating emphasis.
